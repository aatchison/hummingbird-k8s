# Code of Conduct

This is an open-source homelab project exploring Kubernetes on Fedora bootc.
We want it to stay a friendly, low-drama place to learn and collaborate.

## What we expect

- Be civil and constructive in PRs, issues, and discussions.
- Critique code and ideas, not the people behind them.
- Assume good intent, and ask before assuming bad.

## Out of bounds

Harassment, discrimination, slurs, threats, and sustained personal attacks
are not welcome. Maintainers may hide comments, close issues or PRs, or
block contributors who repeatedly cross that line.

## Reporting and decisions

For sensitive matters (security disclosures, interpersonal conflicts), email
the maintainer at `arron.atchison [at] gmail.com`. Expect a reply within a
few business days. Most other things get hashed out in the relevant issue
or PR; when discussion stalls, the maintainer makes the call. This is a
small project, so we lean on common sense over formal process.
